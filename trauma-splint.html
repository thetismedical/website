Intended Use

Night-time splint to ensure the foot is kept in plantar flexion (foot pointed down) for the first 3 months after an Achilles tendon tear.

Instructions

1. Position the splint on the lower shin so that it also covers the top of the foot.


2. The foam of the lower end of the splint should sit just above the joints where the toes meet the foot.


3. Bandage firmly but not too tight.
